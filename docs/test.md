# AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate

Exam Codes: 
AWS-SAA

Prerequisites:
None

Recommended Experience: 
Some experience with AWS is recommended 

Who should take the test:
People looking to learn more about AWS in general

Number of Questions:
60

Test Format:
Multiple Choice 

Length of Test:
80 minutes

Passing Score:
Around 70%

Test Objectives:
https://d1.awsstatic.com/training-and-certification/docs-dev-associate/AWS_certified_developer_associate_blueprint.pdf 

Cost:
150

Registration:
https://www.aws.training/certification?src=dev-assoc 

Expiration:
2 years after the certification is acquired.





Renewal:
Take the recertification exam https://aws.amazon.com/certification/faqs/ 
 OR 
Earn a professional level certification
