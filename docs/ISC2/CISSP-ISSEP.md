# CISSP Information Systems Security Engineering Professional(ISSEP)

## Overview

Exam Code: 
CISSP-ISSEP

Prerequisites:
Must have CISSP with 2 years cumulative paid full-time work experience in
1 or more of the 5 domains of the CISSP-ISSEP CBK.

Recommended Experience: 
5+ years of IT, security, and management experience

Number of Questions:
150

Test Format:
Multiple choice 

Length of Test:
3 Hours

Passing Score:
700/1000

Test Objectives:
[CISSP-ISSEP Objectives](https://www.isc2.org/-/media/ISC2/Certifications/Exam-Outlines/ISSEP-Exam-Outline-v1204---March-2018-Final.ashx)

Cost:
$599

Registration:
[Register Here](https://www.isc2.org/Register-for-Exam#)

Expiration:
3 years after the certification is acquired.

Renewal:
[20 continuing Professional Education (CPE) credits](https://www.isc2.org/-/media/ISC2/Certifications/CPE/CPE---Handbook-Digital-V2.ashx)
