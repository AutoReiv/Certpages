# HealthCare Information Security and Privacy Practitioner(HCISPP)

## Overview

Exam Code: 
HCISPP

Prerequisites:
2 years cumulative work experience in 1 or more knowledge areas of the HCISPP CBK that includes security, compliance and privacy.

Recommended Experience: 
2 years of IT experience

Number of Questions:
125

Test Format:
Multiple choice 

Length of Test:
3 Hours

Passing Score:
700/1000

Test Objectives:
[HCISPP Objectives](https://www.isc2.org/-/media/ISC2/Certifications/Exam-Outlines/HCISPP-Exam-Outline.ashx)

Cost:
$599

Registration:
[Register Here](https://www.isc2.org/Register-for-Exam#)

Expiration:
3 years after the certification is acquired.

Renewal:
[Continuing Professional Education (CPE) credits and annual fees](https://www.isc2.org/-/media/ISC2/Certifications/CPE/CPE---Handbook-Digital-V2.ashx)
