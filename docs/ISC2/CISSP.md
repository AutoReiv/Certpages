# CISSP – Certified Information Systems Security Professional

## Overview

Exam Code: 
CISSP

Prerequisites:
Minimum of five years cumulative paid work experience in two or more of the eight domains or 4 years with a degree

Recommended Experience: 
4 to 5 years of IT experience

Number of Questions:
100-150

Test Format:
Multiple choice [CAT format](https://www.isc2.org/certifications/CISSP/CISSP-CAT#)

Length of Test:
3 Hours

Passing Score:
700/1000

Test Objectives:
[CISSP Objectives](https://www.isc2.org/Certifications/Ultimate-Guides/CISSP?utm_campaign=H-HQ-CISSPultimateguide&utm_source=isc2web&utm_medium=button&utm_content=cissppagebottom#)

Cost:
$699

Registration:
[Register Here](https://www.isc2.org/Register-for-Exam#)

Expiration:
3 years after the certification is acquired.

Renewal:
[Continuing Professional Education (CPE) credits annual fees](https://www.isc2.org/-/media/ISC2/Certifications/CPE/CPE---Handbook-Digital-V2.ashx)
