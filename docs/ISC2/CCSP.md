# Certified Cloud Security Professional(CCSP)

## Overview

Exam Code: 
CCSP

Prerequisites:
5 years cumulative paid work experience in information technology,
of which 3 years must be in information security and 1 year in 1 or more of the 6 domains of the CCSP CBK

Recommended Experience: 
5 years of IT experience

Number of Questions:
125

Test Format:
Multiple choice

Length of Test:
3 Hours

Passing Score:
700/1000

Test Objectives:
[CCSP Objectives](https://www.isc2.org/-/media/ISC2/Certifications/Exam-Outlines/CCSP-Exam-Outline.ashx)

Cost:
$599

Registration:
[Register Here](https://www.isc2.org/Register-for-Exam#)

Expiration:
3 years after the certification is acquired.

Renewal:
[90 continuing Professional Education (CPE) credits annual fees](https://www.isc2.org/-/media/ISC2/Certifications/CPE/CPE---Handbook-Digital-V2.ashx)
