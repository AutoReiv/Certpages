# Systems Security Certified Practitioner(SSCP)

## Overview

Exam Code: 
SSCP

Prerequisites:
Minimum of 1 year cumulative paid work experience in one of seven domain

Recommended Experience: 
One year of IT experience

Number of Questions:
125

Test Format:
Multiple choice 

Length of Test:
3 Hours

Passing Score:
700/1000

Test Objectives:
[SSCP Objectives](https://www.isc2.org/-/media/ISC2/Certifications/Exam-Outlines/SSCP-Exam-Outline-Nov-1-2018.ashx)

Cost:
$249

Registration:
[Register Here](https://www.isc2.org/Register-for-Exam#)

Expiration:
3 years after the certification is acquired.

Renewal:
[60 continuing Professional Education (CPE) credits and annual fees](https://www.isc2.org/-/media/ISC2/Certifications/CPE/CPE---Handbook-Digital-V2.ashx)
