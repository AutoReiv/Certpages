# A+

## Overview

Exam Code: 
220-1001 and 220-1002. Need to pass both exams to earn A+

Prerequisites:
None

Recommended Experience: 
None

Number of Questions:
90

Test Format:
Multiple choice questions (single and multiple response), drag and drops and performance-based 

Length of Test:
90 minutes

Passing Score:
220-1001: 675 (on a scale of 100-900)
220-1002: 700 (on a scale of 100-900)

Test Objectives:
[220-1001 Objectives][1] and [220-1002 Objectives][2]
[1]: https://comptiacdn.azureedge.net/webcontent/docs/default-source/exam-objectives/comptia-a-220-1001-exam-objectives.pdf

[2]: https://comptiacdn.azureedge.net/webcontent/docs/default-source/exam-objectives/comptia-a-220-1002-exam-objectives.pdf

Cost:
$219

Registration:
[Register Here][16]
[16]: https://www.comptia.org/certifications/a#buyoptions

Expiration:
3 years after the certification is acquired.

Renewal:
You can either take a higher certification, pass the most current version of the exam, get 20 or more credits of Continuing Education Units in 3 years, or [complete CertMaster CE, an online, self-paced CE course.][3]
[3]: https://www.comptia.org/continuing-education/choose/renew-with-a-single-activity/complete-a-comptia-certmaster-ce-course#a
