    AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner¶

    Overview¶

    Exam Code: CLF-C01

    Prerequisites: None

    Recommended Experience: None

    Number of Questions: 65

    Test Format: Multiple choice, multiple answer

    Length of Test: 90 minutes

    Passing Score: Around 70%

    Test Objectives: CLF-C01 Objectives

    Cost: $100

    Registration: Register Here

    Expiration: 3 years after the certification is acquired.

    Renewal: Take the Cloud Practitioner exam OR earn and associate-level or professional-level certification
